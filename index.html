About

Brandon Ogrodnik is a 3D artist who enjoys connecting the dots: be it ideas from different disciplines, people from different teams, or pipelines from different industries. Drawling in with having strong technical skills and an academic background in 3D design. The objective and theme of Brandons work is to inspire a transcendent experience of profound beauty, mystery and awe, and provide unique insights into the nature of reality, life and consciousness.
